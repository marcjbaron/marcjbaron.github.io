5.1 - Static Electricity

Part 1: Developing Concepts

In this section we will be investigating the idea of charge. The concept of charge can seem pretty abstract at first, but it becomes more clear when we see how charges interact with each other.

Start by checking off "Show no charges" below the sweater to the left (we can't see charges in the real world!).

If you move the balloon close to the sweater and the wall and release it, you should not notice anything unusual.
